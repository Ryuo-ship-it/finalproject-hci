About Lumpiang Shanghai

Lumpiang Shanghai is a crispy Filipino spring roll filled with seasoned meat. It is a favorite during parties and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • Garlic and onion (minced)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil

Cooking Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine ground pork, minced carrots, and chopped onion.
  2. Add the egg, salt, and pepper, then mix thoroughly.
  3. Lay a lumpia wrapper flat on a clean surface.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling near one edge of the wrapper.
  5. Roll tightly and fold the sides inward to secure the filling.
  6. Seal the edge using a small amount of water.
  7. Repeat the process for the remaining wrappers and filling.
  8. Heat cooking oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  9. Carefully place lumpia into the hot oil.
  10. Fry until golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  11. Remove and drain excess oil using paper towels.
  12. Serve with sweet chili sauce.
