About Tinola

Tinola is a traditional Filipino chicken soup cooked with ginger, green papaya, and chili leaves. It is light, comforting, and healthy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Green Papaya
  • Chili Leaves

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat a small amount of cooking oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté garlic, onion, and sliced ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces and cook until the outer part turns lightly browned.
  4. Pour in water and bring to a boil.
  5. Lower the heat and let it simmer until the chicken becomes tender.
  6. Add green papaya (or chayote) and cook until soft.
  7. Season with fish sauce or salt according to taste.
  8. Add chili leaves (or malunggay leaves) and cook briefly.
  9. Turn off heat and serve hot.
