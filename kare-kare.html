Kare-Kare

A rich Filipino stew cooked in a thick peanut sauce, typically served with vegetables and bagoong for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • Beef or oxtail
  • Peanut butter
  • Vegetables

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil beef or oxtail in a pot until tender.
  2. In another pot, dissolve annatto powder in water.
  3. Add peanut butter and stir until smooth.
  4. Transfer the cooked meat into the peanut sauce.
  5. Add vegetables like eggplant and string beans.
  6. Simmer until vegetables are cooked.
  7. Season according to taste.
  8. Serve with bagoong.
