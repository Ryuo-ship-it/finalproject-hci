About Beef Caldereta

Beef Caldereta is a rich Filipino stew made with tender beef, vegetables, and tomato-based sauce. It is commonly served during celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water or broth
  • 1 cup potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 cup carrots (cubed)
  • Bell peppers
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat cooking oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant and soft.
  3. Add the beef cubes and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in the tomato paste and cook for 1–2 minutes to enhance flavor.
  5. Pour in the tomato sauce and mix well with the beef.
  6. Add water or beef broth, then bring to a gentle boil.
  7. Lower the heat and simmer for 30–40 minutes or until the beef becomes tender.
  8. Add the cubed potatoes and carrots, then cook until vegetables are soft.
  9. Add bell peppers and continue simmering for a few minutes.
  10. Season with salt and pepper according to taste.
  11. Cook for another 3–5 minutes before serving hot.
