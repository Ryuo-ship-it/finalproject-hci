Pork Sinigang

A classic Filipino sour soup made with tender pork and fresh vegetables, known for its comforting and savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Tamarind mix
  • Tomatoes
  • Vegetables

Cooking Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a large pot.
  2. Add pork ribs and cook until meat begins to soften.
  3. Add chopped onions and tomatoes.
  4. Simmer for several minutes to develop flavor.
  5. Add sinigang mix or tamarind seasoning.
  6. Add vegetables such as radish and eggplant.
  7. Continue simmering until vegetables are tender.
  8. Season with salt if needed.
  9. Serve hot.
