About Pancit Bihon

Pancit Bihon is a classic Filipino noodle dish commonly served during birthdays and special occasions. It is made with rice noodles, meat, vegetables, and flavorful seasonings. This dish symbolizes long life and good health.

Ingredients

  • 250g bihon (rice noodles)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 cup sliced chicken or pork
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, cabbage, green beans)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 to 1½ cups broth or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Calamansi or lemon (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Soak the bihon noodles in water for about 10 minutes until softened. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  3. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced chicken or pork. Cook until fully done.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and broth (or water). Stir well.
  6. Add the vegetables and cook until slightly tender.
  7. Add the softened bihon noodles into the pan.
  8. Gently toss and mix to combine all ingredients evenly.
  9. Continue cooking until noodles absorb the liquid and become tender.
  10. Season with salt and pepper according to taste.
  11. Serve warm with calamansi or lemon on the side.
