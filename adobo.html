Chicken Adobo

A traditional Filipino dish where chicken is simmered in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic for a rich and savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves

Cooking Steps

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns in a pot.
  2. Allow the chicken to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Place the pot over medium heat and bring to a gentle boil.
  4. Pour in the vinegar without stirring immediately.
  5. Lower heat and let it simmer for several minutes.
  6. Stir gently and continue cooking until chicken becomes tender.
  7. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Simmer until sauce slightly thickens.
  9. Serve warm with rice.
