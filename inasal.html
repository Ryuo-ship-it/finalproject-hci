Chicken Inasal

A flavorful Filipino grilled chicken marinated in citrus and spices, famous for its smoky taste and juicy texture.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Calamansi juice
  • Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Cooking Steps

  1. In a bowl, prepare the marinade by combining calamansi juice, garlic, soy sauce, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and coat evenly with the marinade.
  3. Cover and marinate for at least 1 hour for better flavor.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  5. Place the marinated chicken on the grill.
  6. Grill while occasionally turning to cook evenly.
  7. Baste with annatto oil to add color and moisture.
  8. Continue grilling until chicken is fully cooked.
  9. Remove from grill and allow to rest briefly.
  10. Serve hot with rice.
